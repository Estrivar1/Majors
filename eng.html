Engineering

Engineering is the application of scientific principles to design and build structures, machines, systems, and processes to solve real-world problems. Engineers work in various fields such as civil, mechanical, electrical, and aerospace engineering, among others.

The median annual salary for engineers varies depending on the specific field. For example, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for civil engineers is around $87,000, while for mechanical engineers, it's around $88,000.
