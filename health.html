Health

Health is the study of physical and mental well-being. It encompasses areas such as healthcare policy, public health, epidemiology, and nutrition. Health professionals work to improve individual and community health through prevention, treatment, and education.

The salary for health professionals varies depending on factors such as job role, specialization, and location. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for healthcare practitioners and technical occupations is around $68,000.
