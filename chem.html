Chemistry

Chemistry is the study of matter, its properties, composition, and interactions. It is a central science that bridges other disciplines such as biology, physics, and environmental science. Chemists work in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, materials science, and environmental protection.

The salary for chemists varies depending on the specific job and industry. For example, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for chemists is around $78,000.
