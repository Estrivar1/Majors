English Literature

English Literature is the study of written works in the English language, including novels, poetry, plays, and essays. It involves critical analysis, interpretation, and appreciation of literary texts from different periods and cultures. English literature graduates may pursue careers in teaching, writing, publishing, or journalism.

The salary for individuals with a degree in English Literature can vary depending on their chosen career path. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for writers and authors is around $63,000.
