Business

Business is the study of how organizations operate and thrive. It involves areas such as management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. Business graduates may pursue careers in various industries, including corporate, finance, consulting, and startups.

The salary for business professionals varies widely depending on factors such as job role, industry, and location. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for business and financial occupations is around $69,000.
