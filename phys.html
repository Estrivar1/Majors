Physics

Physics is the study of matter, energy, space, and time. It seeks to understand the fundamental laws that govern the universe, from the smallest subatomic particles to the largest galaxies. Physicists work in various fields such as research, education, engineering, and finance.

The salary for physicists varies depending on factors such as education, experience, and job location. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for physicists is around $125,000.
