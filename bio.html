Biology

Biology is the study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and their environments. It encompasses various subfields such as genetics, ecology, microbiology, and physiology. Biologists may work in research, healthcare, environmental conservation, or education.

The salary for biologists can vary depending on the specific job and industry. For example, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for biochemists and biophysicists is around $94,000.
